TOUBA

It was implemented in the village of Jahdam in Assiut, where there is a cement brick factory that stopped its activity years ago. The team contributed to reviving the factory again by using an innovative way to produce cement bricks , providing job opportunities for six of the villagers and working on marketing the product.
